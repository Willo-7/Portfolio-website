Royal Gelatin Redesign

This is series of illustration in attepting to redesign Royal Geletin. There is a total of three flavors shown below

This is the orange flavor of gelatin. I kept the original logo the same but changed the roundness of it and the positioning. Normally the entire banner for the logo is fitted entirely in the top but I only placed it in the middle. I also changed the positioning of the label that says the flavor. Its originally on the bottom but this time I placed in near or below the banner for the logo. I also added a sort of ticket that says the serving on the middle left. Lastly, I wanted to change the picture of the Jello. Royal gel looked so serious, it's a food that targets kids and so with that in mind I started drawing on illustrator aiming for something cartoonish. I made a lot of layers and played around with the opacity to make it shiny and look squishy, I then illustrated the Orange on the top of the jello, i used more vibrant colors to it pop. Lastly, i added the square jellos on the side, the plate and the spoon which were kept simple, was played around with different hues of the same color;white.

The cherry was the simples one to make as i made copy of the orange one and just recolored it. The cheery in the other hand had to be illustrated. I used red, white and green to make the cherry. As seen above, the only shaped used was circle, i just reformed it and used it vector points to look more natural.

Similar to the Red and orange. I copied the Orange and recolored it. Then i illustrated the flavor which in this case was the lime. The lime was more like the orange, a lot of highlights and vibrant colors. I only illustrated half or a quarter of a lime that way you can see the inside and can tell the difference. Lastly the type for the flavor was changed.
