Max Verstappen

This poster is of four-time world champion max Verstappen, made in 2023 when I was learning Photoshop and procreate. This poster was a struggle and took approximatly two weeks to make as I wasn't too familiar with the applications. Procreate had an easier learning curve as it is similar to traditional art to some extent but offers a much more customizable amount of colors and brushes to use within a singular application. I used the default brushes already provided in the app. I used two or three different brushes which are Spectra, Tamar, and Syrup. I started off with a sketch drawing out max in a side view. Then i started sketching the f1 car based on his recoveries in the track by drifting. Then i sketched out the track in a different layer. I knew i wanted this poster to pop so i used a vibrant colors throught the poster specially since those are the actual colors for both the track and the red bull team. I added some other small details like the helmet of max. Lastly, I used photoshop for all the typography. For the f1 logo tho i used the offical logo for f1 and placed it at the cornerl

Liam Lawson

This poster is of Liam Lawson, currently a red bull driver after replacing Sergio Perez. This is a poster to hype up the new driver. It took me appoximaly 1 week to do this poster. This poster was made using illustrator. Unlike the Max Verstappen one, this one didnt have a sketch i could already use so i had to make one in the application. With this poster i wanted to capture Liam as the new driver so i had him at the center with a full side profile of the red bull car. Similar to the other poster is has vibrant colors with the RB Labels and sponsors. I also added a plane in the back which was originally suppose to have liam lawsons name in a banner. However i decided o go wih this approch to simplify it

Max Poster part 2

This was an attempt to make use of illustrator more. The process is similar to illustrator but the tools were different in many ways, I used the sketches i had on the original and used it to make this one similar to it. the colors are more darker than the original and there isnt as many details.
