The Natural world; Animal icons

This is simple and fun little website i made just to play around with hover and buttons. I also made illustration of small animals.

Overview

This is the home page showing the word "The Natural World" which also serves as the links to other pages. When you hover  over  "the" it will change color and animation will appear of a flower. When you click "the" it will take you to the gallery of icons. For the Natural world when you hover over "Natural World" it will turn blue and give raining annimation. Once you click it, you will be sent to the page where the icons are scattered in the forest.

Icon map

This is the Icon map where you will see them scattered. They are positioned to their geographic location or the behavior.

Gallery

This is the Gallery page which you will see downloadable icons of small creatures. These are all drawn in illustrator. It took roughly around 3 days to get the icons. There is still more underway! This page will be filled.
